WHO WE ARE

A pioneering edtech solution provider, R&S ACADEMY revolves around a university-industry partnership. It collaborates with premium Indian and global universities to power new-age undergraduate and postgraduate programs through industry-aligned curriculam and advanced technology enablement. R&S ACADEMY implements a holistic knowledge-imparting model to prepare students for new-collar jobs in emerging industries. Its artificial intelligence-based platform assesses the employability of a student and provides necessary interventions.
